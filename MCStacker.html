The new version of MCStacker now has several commands that you wont find on this site. Here's what you can do on the new MCStacker:

  • playsound
  • tellraw
  • title
  • entitydata
  • replaceitem
  • setblock
  • fill
  • summon
  • give
  • testfor

Not only that but you can now also upload and edit supporting mcfunction files with commands that you've saved.

If you havn't checked it out yet, then you should go over to MCStacker.net new site and take a look.
